About Us

Mission

Our Mission is to provide high-quality education that fosters personal growth, critical thinking, and lifelong learning. We are committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of a rapidly changing world.




Vision

Our Vision We envision a community of learners where students are empowered to reach their full potential and become leaders in their chosen fields. HGBaquiran College aims to be recognized for academic excellence, innovative teaching, and impactful community engagement.





History

BREIF INSTITUTIONAL PROFILE Honorato Guzman Baquiran (HGB) College is a private educational institution located in Tumauini, Isabela, Philippines. Established in 2005, the college aims to provide quality education that produces competent and adaptive graduates, equipped to face challenges in both local and global communities. It offers undergraduate programs in various fields such as Teacher Education, Business Administration, Criminology, Information Technology, and Hotel and Restaurant Management. Additionally, the college provides TESDA-accredited short courses in areas like Bread and Pastry Production, Cookery, and Food and Beverage Services The institution is recognized by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) and TESDA, highlighting its commitment to academic excellence and skill development. Its mission revolves around preparing students for professional success and contributing to their personal growth.
